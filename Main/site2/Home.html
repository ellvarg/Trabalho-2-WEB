Services offered at the park

The park is equipped with food and hygiene environments and also has programs for sports activities and animal observation.

Telephone

The park has telephone access points available in five areas.

Food

The park has a restaurant and two snack bars. It is also possible to prepare your own food in the dining area.

WC

The park has accessible restrooms and baby changing facilities. For 5 dollars it is also possible to bathe in the showers.

Camping

The park has a free and equipped camping area for the public.

Fire

There is a designated area inside the camp for fire pits. It is strictly forbidden to make fires outside the designated area due to the risk of forest fire.

Animals

It is possible to observe several species of wild animals during the visit. Feeding the animals is prohibited.

Hiking

The park has guides for hiking with stops at nature observation points.

Swiming

The park has a lake suitable for bathers and divers. There are lifeguards for emergencies.

Fishing

The park has a tank specially reserved for fishing.
